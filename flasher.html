Troubleshooting

  • Browser Support: You must use Chrome, Edge, or Opera on a computer or Android device. iOS and Safari do not support Web Serial.
  • Cable Issues: Ensure you are using a cable capable of data transfer, not just a charging cable.
  • "Failed to initialize" error: If the flasher fails to connect, hold the BOOT button on your CYD, click install, and release the button once the flashing begins.
  • Drivers: If your computer doesn't see the device at all, you may need to install the CH340 or CP210x USB drivers.
